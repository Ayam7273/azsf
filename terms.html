Terms & Condition for Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqat Foundation

Here's some of what's new:

  • Introduction:
    Welcome to the Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqat Foundation. By using our platform, you agree to abide by the terms outlined below. These terms govern the collection, management, and distribution of Zakat and Sadaqat contributions.
  • Definitions:
    Under the GDPR and DPA, we process personal data based on the following lawful grounds;
  • 1. Zakat: An obligatory charity prescribed under Islamic law.
  • 2. Sadaqat: Voluntary charitable donations.
  • 3. Donor: An individual or entity providing Zakat or Sadaqat through the foundation.
  • 4. Beneficiary: An individual or group eligible to receive Zakat or Sadaqat.
  • 5.Foundation: Refers to Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqat Foundation, responsible for managing contributions.
  • Eligibility:
  • 1. Donors must be 18 years or older and provide accurate and lawful information.
  • 2. Beneficiaries must meet the eligibility requirements set out under Shariah guidelines.
  • Donations:
  • 1. Zakat Donations:
  • Contributions will be distributed strictly according to the categories outlined in Islamic law (e.g., the poor, needy, debtors).
  • The foundation guarantees compliance with Shariah principles in Zakat distribution.
  • 2. Sadaqat Donations:
  • Allocated to projects benefiting communities, individuals, or other causes determined by the foundation.
  • 3. Refund Policy:
  • Donations are non-refundable once processed, except for errors or fraud reported within 7 days.
  • Allocation and Use of Funds:
  • The foundation ensures transparent and responsible use of donations.
  • Administrative costs will be disclosed and will not exceed a set percentage of total funds.
  • Detailed annual reports will be published for donor review.
  • Privacy and Data Protection
  • Collection and Use of Data: Donor data, including personal and financial details, is collected only for the purposes of processing donations, issuing receipts, and complying with legal obligations.
  • Compliance with Data Laws:
  • 1. The foundation complies with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and EU GDPR to ensure your data is collected, stored, and processed lawfully and securely.
  • 2. Donors have the right to access, rectify, or erase their data upon request.
  • Data Sharing: Personal data will not be shared with third parties unless required by law or with donor consent
  • Security Measures: Robust security measures are implemented to protect donor data, including encryption and regular audits.
  • Accountability:
  • 1. All fund distributions are overseen by Shariah advisors to ensure compliance with Islamic principles.
  • 2. Independent audits will be conducted annually to maintain transparency and trust.
  • Prohibited Activities:
  • 1. Donors and beneficiaries are prohibited from providing false information or engaging in fraudulent activities.
  • 2. Use of the platform for non-charitable purposes is strictly forbidden.
  • Limitation of Liability:
    The Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqat Foundation will not be liable for:
  • 1. Losses due to incorrect donor-provided information.
  • 2. Delays caused by unforeseen events such as natural disasters.
  • Amendments to Terms:
    The foundation reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions as needed. Donors and beneficiaries will be notified of significant updates via email or our website.
