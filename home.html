Your Zakat brings vital relief to Muslims struggling to make ends meet, providing essential necessities like food and utility payments for families in crisis. With an unprecedented number of Muslims facing hardship, timely support is crucial. Our efforts ensure your Zakat reaches those in need, right here in our community

Hardship Relief

Help Muslims in desperation get through a rough patch by giving them quick access to funds to cover basic living expenses such as food and clothing.

Work

Help Muslims by providing the support they need to find a job and take control of their lives.

Housing

Support vulnerable Muslims in regaining stability and self-sufficiency through secure and affordable accommodation that protects their well-being.

Education

Strengthen the community by investing in the education and development of emerging Muslim religious leaders and other community champions.
