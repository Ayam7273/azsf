Frequently asked questions
  • What is Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqah Foundation?

    Al-Ihsan Zakat and Sadaqah Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to helping those in need by facilitating charitable giving in alignment with Islamic principles.

  • What is the purpose of Al-Ihsan?

    Our purpose is to enable donors to fulfil their charitable obligations while uplifting communities through sustainable programs, emergency relief, and individual support.

  • Who can benefit from the foundation’s support?

    Our aid is directed to vulnerable individuals and communities, including the poor, the hungry, the sick, orphans, and those affected by crises or disasters.

  • Can I choose how my donation is used?

    Yes, you can specify whether your donation supports causes like education, healthcare, food aid, water projects, orphan care, or general relief.

  • Is there a minimum amount for donations?

    There is no minimum donation amount—every contribution makes a difference.

  • How does Al-Ihsan ensure donations are used effectively?

    We prioritize transparency and accountability by vetting beneficiaries, conducting regular audits, and providing donors with detailed reports on the impact of their contributions.

  • Does Al-Ihsan accept non-monetary donations?

    Yes, we accept in-kind donations, such as food, clothing, and medical supplies, for specific campaigns. Please contact us for details.

  • Can I donate anonymously?

    Yes, we respect donor privacy and allow for anonymous contributions.

  • Are my donations tax-deductible?

    In most countries, donations to Al-Ihsan are tax-deductible. We provide receipts to assist with your tax filings.

  • Can I donate on behalf of someone else?

    Yes, you can donate in honour of a loved one, whether they are living or deceased.

  • How does Al-Ihsan select beneficiaries?

    We conduct thorough needs assessments to identify individuals and communities most in need, ensuring aid reaches the right recipients.

  • Does Al-Ihsan charge administrative fees?

    A small portion of donations may be allocated to cover administrative costs to ensure operational efficiency, unless specified otherwise.
