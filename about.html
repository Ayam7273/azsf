Our Vision

What we want to achieve?

With unwavering faith and the profound desire to serve humanity guided by the divine teachings of Islam, we the founders, and members of the Al-Ihsan Zakat & Sadaqat Foundation, solemnly establish this Constitution. Inspired by the Quranic verses and Hadiths, we commit to creating an organization that embodies the essence of compassion, charity, and social justice, seeking to be a radiant beacon of hope and transformation for the world.

Eradicating Poverty with Compassion

A world where compassion and charity eradicate poverty and ensure dignity for every individual.

Building Empowered Communities

To be a global beacon of Islamic charity, leading the way in eradicating poverty and creating sustainable, empowered communities.

Faith-Driven Compassion

To uphold the teachings of the Quran and Hadiths by fostering a culture of compassion and charity that uplifts marginalized communities.

Empowerment Through Giving

To provide financial assistance and empowerment programs through Zakat and Sadaqat, enabling individuals to achieve dignity and self-reliance.

Championing Social Justice

To advance social justice by addressing poverty, inequality, and vulnerability through sustainable and impactful charitable initiatives.

Spreading Kindness Globally

To serve humanity by promoting acts of kindness, generosity, and care in accordance with Islamic principles.

Transforming Lives with Compassion

To transform lives by delivering relief, development, and support programs rooted in the divine values of compassion and social responsibility.

Mobilizing Communities for Impact

To inspire and mobilize communities globally to engage in acts of giving and service that reflect the essence of Islamic charity.
